The App may collect location data while running in the background solely for the purpose of detecting parking spot departures. ## 6. Physical Activity Recognition

The App uses your device's activity recognition capabilities to detect transitions between walking and driving. This is used solely to trigger the departure detection feature.

- Activity data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to our servers
- This feature can be disabled at any time via **Settings → Departure Detection**
- Revoking the Activity Recognition permission via your device settings will disable departure detection but will not affect other features of the App

## 7. Background Services

The App runs a background service to support departure detection. This service:

- Monitors device activity and location to detect when you leave a parking spot
- Displays a notification prompting you to confirm whether you have vacated a spot
- Submits a parking report only upon your explicit confirmation
- Can be fully disabled via **Settings → Departure Detection**

You may terminate the background service at any time without affecting your account or other App functionality.
